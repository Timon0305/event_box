FAQs

What is Event Box? What is the benefit for using this platform?

We are an online marketplace that connects pre-vetted vendors with planners to streamline any event. Event Box was founded by event industry veterans a leader in the Event Industry with a team of industry veterans.

What is the history of Event Box?

Founded in 2019, Event Box has been years in the making. Liron David and his team were always asked by clients, friends and work acquaintances for vendor suggestions and recommendations for creative products for multiple locations. The team has taken their passion for finding the coolest innovative products and services and connecting all planners to be able to source the newest event trends.

What event types does Event Box serve?

Event Box works across an array of events, from weddings to corporate meetings, even product launches.

What markets does Event Box serve?

Currently Event Box services the Metropolitan NY area, however we are rapidly expanding to other US Cities.

Sign up for our newsletter and follow our Social Media for updates!

Who is considered a “Planner”?

Anyone that is looking to book products and services from vendors for any type of event.

Some planner types include: Event Planners of all kinds - Agency, Company Internal, Third Party, Social Party Planner, Event Venues, DMC Planners.

I’m planning an event in NY but I am based in another country, can I use Event Box?

Yes! Please note all pricing is in USD.

If I am a planner and I need to send a certificate of insurance or provide floor plans or other important documents, what do I need to do?

You can attach the items in the message section when communicating with the vendor(s).

What is the cancellation policy after a quote is paid for by the planner?

A planner has 24 hours after paying for an order to cancel it. If there are any issues contact info@myeventbox.com.

If I am the planner, who can I contact if I have a question about an item before I submit a quote request?

You can message the vendor on the product/service micro page.

How do I become a vendor on Event Box?

If you are a new vendor you would need to click on the vendor sign up link.

The Event Box team will review your company information and will either accept or reject your business.

As a vendor, how do I provide quotes to planners?

A planner initiates a quote request therefore you will be alerted by email and if logged in you will receive a notification.

You then have the opportunity to fill out a quote form and send it back to the planner through the Event Box platform or you can reject the request for a quote.

What are the fees involved?

If you are a planner, the service is free of charge!

If you are a vendor, Event Box charges a transaction fee.
