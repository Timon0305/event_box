About Us

Event Box is an online marketplace connecting planners and vendors to streamline any event.

We support event industry professionals and service/product providers with maximum business exposure and event management tools creating a seamless marketplace.

Event Box connects vetted vendors to various event planners through our online platform that saves the planner time and effort when organizing and booking events.

How does it work?

PLANNERS
  • From start to finish you create a detailed event page for a single event to multiple events.
  • Search from the curated inventory for all event categories that you would need for your event to make it one of a kind.
  • Message + Communicate with a vendor about a specific product.
  • Request a quote based on your personalized event needs.
  • Submit payment for the confirmed quotes. (no more credit card forms to fill out for multiple vendors for one event)
  • Have a successful event!
VENDORS
  • Create a personalized profile and upload those top rated and specialty products and services with all details and photos available.
  • Once a planner requests a quote or submits for an inquiry for a specific item, you will be notified and the channel will open up for communication.
  • Once a quote is submitted for pricing and availability, you have a timed request that is active.
  • You can either reject the request (which requires reasoning - i.e not available that date) or you can submit a quote and fill out all necessary notes, including additional pricing that will match that specific event needs.
  • The planner will then have to accept the pricing on the quote and submit for payment, reject the quote based on the new pricing or request a counter where you will have the opportunity to adjust items on the quote.
  • The planner will submit payment and you are all set.
  • Have a successful event!
